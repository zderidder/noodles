Who are we?

We are two students from Dr. Gomes' 268 Web Development course. We wanted to make a website for people who want to eat or cook noodles in Eau Claire, or for anybody who wanted to learn more about the history of noodles.

Why noodles?

As most noodles are just a combination of something like flour and water, they are usually very inexpensive and simple dishes (although toppings and sides can add complexity and cost). A basic noodle dish is not only cost effective and tasty, but there are plenty of different styles with different flavors and textures.

Connor

Zach

I am also a CS major. I enjoy eating pasta and different types of noodles, particularly spaghetti with meatballs and ramen noodles. I also wanted to learn some more about Eau Claire restaurants that offered noodles, which gave us some passion for this site.
