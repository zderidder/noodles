Our fun facts

The word noodle

The word noodle derives from the German word nudel, which means "dumpling" in German.

National noodle day

October 6th is National Noodle day in the United States of America. This day is honored by many on sites such as Instagram by using the tag #NationalNoodleDay

Instant noodles luxurious past

Instant noodles nowadays are staple foods of college students and inexpensive dining, but they were once in fact a luxury item in the early ages.

Space noodles?

The first type of noodle to ever be consumed in space was Top Ramen, by the astronaut Soichi Noguchi in 2005.

Noodle etiquette

In Japan, slurping your noodles is actually not seen in poor taste. It means that you were thoroughly enjoying your appreciation for the host and the deliciousness of the meal.
