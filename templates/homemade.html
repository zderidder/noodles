Making The Noodles Themselves

It is worth noting that making noodles can be a difficult task, especially depending on the type of noodles and the equipment they require. However, if you do want to try making some yourself, the Spruce Eats has a simple recipe for egg noodles.

Egg Noodles

Notes:

You can find the recipe here.

This recipe, as you might expect, contains eggs.

Information:

This is a somewhat simple recipe for some simple egg noodles. It takes a total of 70 minutes to make, and it gives 4 servings.

These noodles can be used in a variety of dishes such as soups, stews, or pasta. If you replace dried noodles with these fresh ones, they should be cooked for less time.

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour

1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt

2 large eggs
