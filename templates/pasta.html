One-Pot Garlic Parmesan Pasta

This pasta is made using one pot in a short time, so it is perfect for dorm cooking! It does include a few fresh ingredients, which can be skipped without harming the structure of the dish. All of the other ingredients can be stored for longer periods of time.

Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Notes:

You can find the recipe here.

This recipe contains milk.

Information:

This is a simple recipe for making a quick pasta. It takes a total of 30 minutes to make, and it gives 4 servings.

As long as you keep an eye on the noodles (as they will likely cook faster), you could easily cut the recipe into quarters to make a personal dish.

Key Ingredients:

4 cloves minced garlic

2 cups chicken broth

8 ounces uncooked fettuccine

1/4 cup freshly grated parmsean cheese

2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Spaghetti

Spaghetti has a few adaptions that are the most simple and common forms of pasta. This recipe is no exception, as it says it will be done in less than 20 minutes.

Easy Spaghetti

Notes:

You can find the recipe here.

This recipe asks for specific spaghetti noodles.

Information:

This recipe is a standard - add onions, peppers, and fresh herbs and use whatever pasta noodles you have to create this simple spaghetti dish. It takes a total of 15 minutes to make, and it gives 4-6 servings.

The recipe reccomends Barilla spaghetti noodles as it takes less time, but any noodles will work as long as the water is brought to a boil before adding.

Key Ingredients:

12 ounces of spaghetti

1 pound of ground beef

24 tomato sauce

Optional chopped basil

Optional grated parmesan cheese
