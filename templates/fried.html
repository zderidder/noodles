Fried Noodle Dishes

Pad Thai Gai

Pad Thai Gai is a Thai dish that includes Thai rice noodles that are stir fried with chicken.

Pad Thai Gai

Notes:

You can find the recipe here.

This recipe reccomends nuts, which can be excluded.

Information:

This is a simple recipe for making a quick pasta. It takes a total of 30 minutes to make, and it gives 4 servings.

As long as you keep an eye on the noodles (as they will likely cook faster), you could easily cut the recipe into quarters to make a personal dish.

Key Ingredients:

8 ounces Thai rice noodles

1/2 pound chicken breast

various vegetables, eggs, and herbs

various sauces for stir fry sauce

Shanghai Fried Noodles

Shanghai Cu Chao Mian, known as Shanghai fried noodles in the United States, is a popular chinese fried noodle dish with pork.

Shanghai Fried Noodles

Notes:

You can find the recipe here.

This is a very quick recipe!

Information:

This is a simple recipe with a relatively straightforward set of instructions, but they do include options for vegetarians. It takes a total of 20 minutes to make, and it gives 4 servings.

Although it doesn't state it, you could easily replace the pork with a vegetarian protien and follow similar steps.

Key Ingredients:

12 lean pork

8 shiitake mushrooms

1 pound Udon noodles

various sauces and additions
