Better Ramen

The most common form of japanese ramen is instant ramen, using either a cup or package. Although this isn't exactly a recipe, the instant ramen can be improved with this easy guide.

Instant Ramen Upgrade

Notes:

You can find the recipe here.

Check out the guide instead of the recipe if you just want some tips.

Information:

This is a guide as well as a recipe. If you want to improve your meal, even just one tip would. It takes a total of 10 minutes to make, and it gives 1-2 servings.

Their recipe is pretty far down on their page.

The broth could be exchanged for another broth - it doesn't have to be vegetable broth.

Key Ingredients:

1 pack instant ramen

1 cup vegetable broth

1 cup water

many small, optional additions

Pho

Pho is a vietnamese noodle soup with meat and vegetables. This recipe simplifies the well known restaurant version.

Simple Pho

Notes:

You can find the recipe here.

The protien can be swapped for other options.

Information:

This recipe lists a lot of different customizeable parts, and even has support for a vegan recipe. It takes 1 hour and 15 minutes to make, and it gives 2-4 servings.

The yield is 2 large bowls, or 4 medium sized bowls.

Pho broth has a lot of ingredients, and will be a majority of the effort for this recipe.

Key Ingredients:

steak, chicken, pork, or shrimp

thin rice noodles

pho broth

various toppings
